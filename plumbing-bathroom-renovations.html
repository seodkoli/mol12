Frequently Asked Questions

Can a plumber repair a water heater?

Yes, plumbers are skilled in diagnosing and repairing issues with water heaters, including problems with heating elements, thermostats, pressure relief valves, and leaking tanks.

How do I know if I have a hidden water leak?

Signs of a hidden water leak include unexplained increases in water bills, water stains on walls or ceilings, damp or musty odors, and the sound of running water when no fixtures are in use. If you suspect a hidden leak, it's essential to call a plumber for inspection and repair.

What qualifications do plumbers have?

Plumbers typically undergo vocational training, apprenticeships, and licensing exams to acquire the necessary skills and certifications required to work in the plumbing profession.
